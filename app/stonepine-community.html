Stonepine
Stonepine is a luxurious and gated residential community located just west of Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Known for its upscale properties, private setting, and exclusive amenities, Stonepine offers an opulent and secluded living experience.
  • Estate Properties

    Stonepine is characterized by its lavish estate homes and upscale properties, often situated on spacious lots, providing residents with a sense of privacy and opulence.

  • Gated Community

    Stonepine is a gated neighborhood, offering a high level of security and exclusivity for its residents.

  • Natural Beauty

    The neighborhood is surrounded by picturesque landscapes, including rolling hills, forests, and natural vistas, offering opportunities for outdoor activities and enjoying nature.

  • Exclusive Lifestyle

    Stonepine offers an exclusive and refined living environment, appealing to those seeking a sophisticated and tranquil way of life.

  • Amenities

    The community features amenities such as private clubhouses, tennis courts, and recreational areas that cater to residents' leisure and entertainment needs.

  • Architectural Excellence

    Stonepine showcases impeccable architectural designs and upscale landscaping, contributing to its luxurious character.

  • Community Spirit

    The neighborhood fosters a sense of community among its residents through exclusive events, gatherings, and social activities.

  • Educational Opportunities

    Families have access to nearby schools, both public and separate, ensuring quality education for children within the community.

  • Scenic Views

    Many homes in Stonepine offer stunning views of the surrounding landscapes and the nearby Rocky Mountains.

  • Transportation

    Stonepine's location provides easy access to major roadways, allowing residents to commute to other parts of Calgary and beyond.

Stonepine offers an upscale and exclusive living environment with a strong emphasis on luxury properties, security, and a refined lifestyle. Its estate homes, private amenities, and serene setting make it an attractive and prestigious destination for those seeking a high-end and secluded living experience near Calgary.
