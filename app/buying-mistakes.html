10 Biggest Mistakes to Avoid When Buying a Home
  • 1. Not getting a detailed market analysis from your real estate agent to determine the home's value
  • 2. Failing to conduct a property inspection before purchasing
  • 3. Neglecting to obtain a Real Property Report (RPR) with compliance for the property
  • 4. Making a low-ball offer that discourages the seller from negotiating with you
  • 5. Not getting pre-qualified for a mortgage before making an offer
  • 6. Not allowing enough time to gather important information like building inspections, permits, title examinations, and municipal/community details
  • 7. Failing to work with a Buyer's Agent who can represent your interests
  • 8. Purchasing a home directly from a For-Sale-By-Owner or Builder without a real estate professional and attorney
  • 9. Buying a property without obtaining title insurance
  • 10. Not getting a City Inspection or copies of relevant permits

While this list doesn't cover all potential pitfalls, these are common mistakes to watch out for. Choosing the right real estate agent can help you avoid these issues.
