Edgemont
Edgemont is a well-established residential community located in the northwest quadrant of Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Known for its scenic location, abundant green spaces, and family-friendly atmosphere, Edgemont offers a balanced and suburban living experience.
  • Natural Beauty

    Edgemont is surrounded by picturesque landscapes, including Nose Hill Park to the east, offering residents opportunities for outdoor activities and scenic views.

  • Family-Focused

    The neighborhood features parks, playgrounds, and recreational areas that cater to families, providing safe spaces for children to play and families to gather.

  • Educational Opportunities

    Families have access to nearby schools, both public and separate, ensuring quality education for children within the community.

  • Transportation

    Edgemont's location offers convenient access to major roadways, allowing for easy commuting to other parts of Calgary and access to urban amenities.

  • Community Spirit

    The neighborhood hosts various events and programs that promote a sense of community, encouraging neighbors to connect and engage.

  • Variety of Housing

    Edgemont offers a range of housing options, including single-family homes, townhouses, and condominiums, catering to different preferences and budgets.

  • Scenic Views

    Many homes in Edgemont offer views of the surrounding natural landscapes and the Rocky Mountains, adding to the visual appeal of the community.

  • Convenient Amenities

    Residents have access to nearby shopping centers, restaurants, and services, enhancing overall convenience.

  • Outdoor Recreation

    Edgemont offers residents opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and exploring the nearby parks and pathways.

  • Community Facilities

    Edgemont features its own community center and facilities that host a variety of programs and activities.

Edgemont offers a suburban and family-friendly living environment with a strong emphasis on outdoor recreation, community engagement, and convenient amenities. Its natural beauty, range of housing options, and community-focused atmosphere make it an inviting and well-rounded neighborhood for individuals and families seeking a balanced lifestyle in Calgary.
